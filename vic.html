Victoria

The victoria memorial hall is a large marbel building on the maidan in the central kolkata,built between 1906 and 1921.It's dedicated to the memory of Queen Victoria,empress of India from 1876 to 1901. The largest monument anywhere in the world,it stands in 64 acres of gardens and now a museam under the control of ministry of culture
